Best Video Editing Software for YouTube (For Every Budget)

By SooperBlooper Team•July 18, 2025•9 min read

Choosing the right video editing software can feel overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. The "best" software is the one that fits your budget, skill level, and computer's capabilities. This guide breaks down the top choices for every type of YouTube creator.

The Best Free Option: DaVinci Resolve

For creators on a budget, DaVinci Resolve is the undisputed champion. The free version is a full-fledged professional editing suite used on Hollywood films. It offers incredibly powerful color grading, effects, and audio tools that surpass many paid options. While it has a steeper learning curve, the investment in learning it pays off immensely.

The Best for Beginners: CapCut (Desktop)

Originally a mobile app, CapCut's desktop version is perfect for beginners. Its intuitive interface, built-in text effects, auto-captions, and access to a library of royalty-free music make it incredibly easy to create stylish, engaging videos with minimal effort. This is a great choice if you want to get started quickly. You can learn more in our guide on how to add music to your videos.

The Industry Standards (Paid)

  • Adobe Premiere Pro: The go-to choice for most professional YouTubers. It's powerful, versatile, and integrates seamlessly with other Adobe apps like Photoshop and After Effects. It operates on a monthly subscription model.
  • Final Cut Pro: If you're a Mac user, Final Cut Pro is a fantastic option. It's known for its speed, ease of use, and powerful performance on Apple hardware. It's a one-time purchase, which can be more cost-effective in the long run.

Conclusion: Don't Obsess Over Software

Remember, the software is just a tool. A great story edited on free software will always beat a boring story edited on an expensive suite. Start with a free option like DaVinci Resolve or CapCut, focus on learning storytelling and pacing, and only upgrade when you feel the software is limiting your creativity.
