How to Get More Views on YouTube: 10 Proven Ways

By SooperBlooper Team•July 20, 2025•8 min read

If you want to grow your channel, getting more views isn’t about luck—it’s about strategy. Here are 10 actionable ways to boost your YouTube video views, even if you’re just starting out.

1. Use Strong Titles and Thumbnails

  • Put main keywords at the front of your video titles for SEO.
  • Bright, custom thumbnails increase click-through.

2. Optimize Metadata and Descriptions

  • Include your target keywords in video descriptions and tags.
  • Add relevant YouTube tags and detailed summaries.

3. Use Playlists to Keep Viewers Watching

  • Organize videos by topic in related playlists for more sessions per viewer.

4. Link Videos Together

  • Use cards and end screens to keep viewers on your channel longer.

5. Promote Your Videos

  • Share every video on social media—see sharing strategies.
  • Post in relevant Reddit threads, Facebook groups, and your email list.

6. Jump on Trends

  • Use Google Trends, explore trending YouTube topics, or react to the latest news in your niche.

7. Hook Your Audience Early

  • Nail your intro—see engaging video intro tips—to boost watch time and recommendations.

8. Upload Consistently

  • Stick to a realistic schedule (posting frequency guide).

9. Collaborate with Other Creators

  • Swap shoutouts and collaborate—see cross-promotion strategies.

10. Analyze and Improve

  • Check YouTube Analytics for top-performing topics and adjust future content.
