Best Time to Post on YouTube: Maximize Your Reach

By SooperBlooper Team•July 20, 2025•6 min read

YouTube’s algorithm prioritizes videos that get strong engagement right after being published. Here’s how to choose the best time for maximum reach!

What Data Should You Check?

  • Go to YouTube Studio → Analytics → Audience tab
  • Check “When your viewers are on YouTube” heatmap for your channel
  • Test different days/times and watch early view spikes in Analytics

Best Practices from Top Creators

  • Weekday evenings (6–9pm local time) are often top for most channels
  • For international audiences, experiment to discover the sweet spot for time zones
  • Schedule videos about 1–2 hours before your traffic peak

Don’t Forget Consistency

  • Publishing videos at the same time builds anticipation and habits
  • Let subscribers know your schedule through your channel banner or Community Tab
