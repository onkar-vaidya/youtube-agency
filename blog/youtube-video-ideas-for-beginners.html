10 YouTube Video Ideas for Beginners (Popular & Easy to Start)

By SooperBlooper Team•July 18, 2025•8 min read

The hardest part of starting a YouTube channel is often figuring out what to talk about. The good news is, you don't need a groundbreaking idea for your first few videos. Here are 10 popular and easy-to-execute video ideas perfect for beginners to get the ball rolling.

10 Easy Video Ideas to Kickstart Your Channel

  1. Introduction Video: A short video introducing yourself, your channel, and what viewers can expect. It's a simple way to get started.
  2. A "Day in the Life" Vlog: Show a typical day in your life or related to your niche (e.g., "A Day in the Life of a Software Developer").
  3. Product Review: Review a product you know and love (or hate). It could be a tech gadget, a book, or a coffee machine.
  4. "How-To" Tutorial: Teach something you know well. This could be anything from "how to bake sourdough bread" to "how to use a specific software feature."
  5. Unboxing Video: Unbox a new product and give your first impressions. This format is consistently popular.
  6. Q&A Video: Answer frequently asked questions about your niche or profession.
  7. Behind-the-Scenes: Show viewers a peek behind the curtain of your work or creative process.
  8. Listicle ("Top 5"): Create a list-based video, like "Top 5 Mistakes Beginners Make in [Your Niche]."
  9. Reaction Video: React to a popular video, movie trailer, or event in your niche.
  10. Share Your Story: Tell the story of how you got started in your field or with your hobby.
