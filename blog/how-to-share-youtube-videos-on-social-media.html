How to Share YouTube Videos on Social Media: Platform Tips

By SooperBlooper Team•July 20, 2025•7 min read

Simply dropping a YouTube link is not enough to grow your audience on other platforms. Here’s how to maximize cross-platform engagement and turn social followers into YouTube subscribers!

Instagram

  • Post a 15–60 sec highlight as a Reel with a call to watch the full video (see YouTube Shorts tips).
  • Use Stories with swipe-up (if eligible) or "link in bio" prompts.
  • Pair eye-catching visuals, captions, and trending audio.

TikTok

  • Edit your most viral, funny, or value-packed segment as a native TikTok video.
  • Engage with comment replies to boost engagement.
  • Put YouTube channel or video links in your TikTok bio (linktr.ee works great).

Facebook

  • Share your video natively in Facebook Groups related to your niche (honor group rules).
  • Post on your profile and Facebook Pages with an enticing question or teaser text.

Pro Tips Across Platforms

  • Always tailor your message to each platform and audience.
  • Follow up on comments to drive cross-platform conversation.
  • Try different posting times using insights from best YouTube post times.
