How to Create a YouTube Playlist: Boost Watch Time

By SooperBlooper Team•July 18, 2025•6 min read

YouTube Playlists are the easiest way to guide viewers from one video to the next, boost your watch time, and organize your content library for a better subscriber experience. Here’s a quick how-to and playlist strategy guide!

How to Create a Playlist (Step by Step)

  1. Go to YouTube Studio and click Content from the left menu.
  2. Select Playlists at the top, then click New playlist.
  3. Give your playlist a keyword-rich title and description so it can be discovered via search!
  4. Add relevant public videos (your own or others’).
  5. Set the order: chronological, by popularity, or custom.

Playlist Tips for Growth

  • Group related topics (e.g., "Beginner YouTube Tips" or "Video SEO Series").
  • Link playlists in end screens—see how to use end screens.
  • Share playlist links on social media or your website.
  • Keep series or tutorials in the right logical order for binge-watching.

Why Playlists Matter

  • Increase session duration—the #1 YouTube growth metric.
  • Help YouTube suggest your own videos after one finishes.
  • Make your channel easier to navigate for new viewers.
