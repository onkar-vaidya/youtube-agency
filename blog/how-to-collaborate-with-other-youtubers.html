How to Collaborate with Other YouTubers: The Power of Cross-Promotion

By SooperBlooper Team•July 20, 2025•7 min read

Teaming up with other creators is one of the fastest ways to grow your audience, share new ideas, and reach viewers who may never have found your channel. Here’s how to make collabs work for everyone involved.

Why Collaborations Work

  • Tap into each other’s audiences for instant exposure
  • Fresh perspectives and new content formats
  • Community building and credibility

Finding the Right Collaboration Partner

  • Look for creators with overlapping audiences but non-identical content
  • Size doesn’t always matter—value and synergy beat sub counts
  • Engage genuinely on their videos before reaching out

How to Pitch a Collaboration

  • Be specific (“Let’s swap guest appearances on our ‘React to Trends’ video series!”)
  • Give mutual benefit (“Your tech reviews + my editing hacks = more value for both audiences”)
  • Keep it friendly and professional

Collab Video Ideas

  • Dual Q&A or challenge videos
  • Guest appearances
  • Comparing opinions on trending topics
  • Playlist swaps or mutual video promotions
