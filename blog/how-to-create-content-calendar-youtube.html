How to Create a YouTube Content Calendar

By SooperBlooper Team•July 18, 2025•7 min read

Consistent growth on YouTube starts with a plan. A content calendar helps you brainstorm, organize, and execute your video ideas without stress or last-minute panic. Here’s how to build one that works!

Why Use a Content Calendar?

  • Prevents burnout by spreading video ideas ahead of time
  • Makes room for trends, holidays, and collaborations
  • Helps you batch film and edit, saving time

Building Your Calendar in 4 Steps

  1. Decide Your Upload Frequency: Use a realistic schedule (see how often to post on YouTube).
  2. Brainstorm Video Ideas: Refer to your master list of video ideas.
  3. Map Content for the Month:
    • Assign each idea a date on Google Calendar, Notion, or a spreadsheet.
    • Mix repeating formats (tutorial, reaction, challenge, etc.).
  4. Add Deadlines for Production: Note when scripts, filming, editing, and uploads are due.

Templates & Tools

  • Google Calendar: Simple and shareable.
  • Notion: Great for teams or solo creators who love organization.
  • Spreadsheets: Still work! Add columns for title, status, publish date, and notes.

Review and Adjust

Revisit your content calendar every few weeks. Don’t be afraid to swap ideas or move things if inspiration (or news) strikes!
